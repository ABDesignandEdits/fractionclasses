Preferred Exams

CBSE

The CBSE Board Exams are national-level assessments conducted annually for Classes 10 and 12, evaluating students' academic performance and determining eligibility for higher education opportunities in India.

TBSE

TBSE exams are annual board examinations conducted for Class 10 (Madhyamik) and Class 12 (HS) students in Tripura, assessing their academic knowledge and eligibility for higher education.

JEE

JEE (Joint Entrance Examination) is a highly competitive national-level exam for aspirants seeking admission to undergraduate engineering programs (B.Tech/B.E.) at premier institutions like IITs, NITs, and other renowned colleges.

NEET UG

NEET UG (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test for Undergraduate) is a national-level entrance exam in India for aspirants seeking admission to MBBS, BDS, and other medical courses in government and private institutions.
